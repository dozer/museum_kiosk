Our New Terminal System is Live!

Thanks to the great student software development team at Rowan University our new terminal system is live to better assist you in enjoying our museum! The new terminal system is incredibly easy to use and is a vast improvement on our old terminal system which was slow and out-dated. We are delighted to have our new terminal system and hope that it will assist you in further enjoyment of our museum!

Exploring our Exhibits

To view our vast collection of thrilling exhibits simply navigate to the 'Exhibits' page. This can be done by clicking the 'Exhibits' link at the top or bottom of the terminal.

Finding our Exhibits

If a specific museum peaks your interests and you want to view it in person but don't know where it is, don't worry, we've got you covered. To find an exhibit in the museum and get directions to it just navigate to the 'Map' page. This can be done by clicking the 'Map' link at the top or bottom of the terminal.

Learning More About the M.C.H.

Interested in learning more about the Museum of Computer History itself? No problem! More information can be found by clicking the 'About' link at the top or bottom of the terminal.

Contacting the Museum

If you want to contact the Museum of Computer History for any reason at all, click the 'Contact Us' link at the top or bottom of the terminal.

Everything Else

If you are interested in some basic knowledge, further reading, or computer scientists who we don't have on display, please refer to the 'Useful Links' section on the right hand side of the terminal. For any other assistance please don't hesitate to ask any of the museum staff or the front desk; we are more than happy to help!
