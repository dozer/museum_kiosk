Our Mission

The mission of the Computer History Museum is to preserve and keep alive the great achievements and advancements in computing. In a field that changes so rapidly it is important to be able to look back through history and appreciate where we came from and how we got where we are today. We are dedicated to celebrating the history of computing and offer a wide array of exhibits ranging from prominent computer scientists to world-changing inventions. No matter who you are, you will find something of interest at the Museum of Computer History!

Who We Are

The Museum of Computer History is a nonprofit organization dedicated to exploring the history of computing. We have done our absolute best to foster an environment to learn and have fun for all. Our tireless staff has scoured the globe ensuring that all notable events, persons, and inventions have been thoroughly covered within the museum. If you are interested in helping out the museum or have an idea for a new exhibit, please let our staff at the front desk know or leave us a message!
